# **Customer Churn Analysis: Stopping the Silent Revenue Killer**

> **Every churned customer is revenue walking out the door. But *why* are they leaving? Is it the service? The price? Comparison with competitors?**

## **The Challenge**

Businesses often react to customer loss only after it happens. By then, it's too late. The goal of this project was to shift from a **reactive** approach to a **proactive** strategy. We set out to answer critical questions:
- *Who* is most likely to leave?
- *When* are they at the highest risk?
- *What* can we do to make them stay?

---

## **The Journey: From Data to Strategy**

Using **Power BI Desktop** and **Excel**, we analyzed historical customer data to uncover hidden patterns. We didn't just look at churn rates; we looked at **behavior**. 

### **1. The Investigation (Analysis & Modeling)**
We built predictive models to identify at-risk customers before they cancel. This involved:
- **Segmentation**: Grouping customers not just by demographics, but by shared risk characteristics.
- **Root Cause Analysis**: Identifying the specific pain points driving customers away.

### **2. The Discovery (Key Insights)**
The data revealed striking patterns. Certain cohorts showed clear early warning signs long before they actually churned.

## **Turning Insights into Action**

Data is useless without communication. The final piece of the puzzle was translating complex analytics into clear business directives.

### **Strategic Impact:**
1.  **Reduce Customer Loss**: We now identify at-risk customers early, allowing for timely retention offers.
2.  **Improve Satisfaction**: By understanding pain points, we improved the service areas that mattered most.
3.  **Data-Driven Decisions**: We replaced "gut feeling" with hard evidence.

### **Communication That Drives Change:**
Beyond the dashboard, I ensure stakeholders understand the "So What?" through clear, tailored communication.
